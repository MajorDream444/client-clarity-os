# Proposal Generator Prompt

Draft a human-review proposal from an approved Client Clarity Blueprint.

## Inputs

- Client record
- Approved blueprint output
- Assets and data room notes
- Build track
- Human pricing notes
- Human scope notes

## Rules

- Do not invent pricing, payment terms, outcomes, deadlines, or guarantees.
- Use human-provided pricing and scope only.
- Keep the proposal focused on the first useful build.
- Separate current proof from future opportunity.
- Include what is explicitly out of scope.
- Preserve client-specific language without overclaiming.
- End with a clear next step tied to Stripe payment stage only if the payment link is provided.

## Sections

1. Context
2. What We Heard
3. Diagnosis
4. Recommended First Build
5. Scope
6. Out of Scope
7. Timeline
8. Investment
9. Payment Stage
10. Next Step
