# Notion Template Generator Prompt

Create or refine a Notion intake template for Client Clarity Intake OS.

## Constraints

- Keep the template premium, specific, and client-facing.
- Do not make it feel like a generic survey.
- Keep archetype language separate:
  - Creator-Healer: founder story, healing work, doctrine, audience, visibility, paid proof, front door, community, retreat/workshop possibility.
  - Shakti System: doctrine, lineage, Shakti Tantra, somatics, neuroscience, diaspora positioning, women-centered sovereignty, circles, retreats, sanctuary, temple pathway.
  - Media Engine: founder story, PR/media proof, cultural authority, service packaging, verticals, recurring revenue, campaign revenue, fundability, acquisition potential.
- Do not make medical claims.
- Do not invent client facts.

## Required Sections

- Origin
- Work / Authority
- People / ICP
- Offer / Revenue
- Resistance / Boundaries
- Expression / Delivery
- Vision
- Reality Check
- Final Reflection

## Output

Return a clean markdown template ready to paste into Notion.
