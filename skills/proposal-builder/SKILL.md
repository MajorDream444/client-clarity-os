# Skill: Proposal Builder

## Purpose

Draft proposals from approved Client Clarity Blueprint Outputs.

## Inputs

- Approved blueprint output.
- Client record.
- Build track.
- Human pricing notes.
- Human scope notes.

## Rules

- Do not draft from unapproved AI output.
- Do not invent pricing, guarantees, proof, or timelines.
- Keep the first build clear and contained.
- Include what is out of scope.
- Stripe links or payment stages may be referenced only when provided.
- Final proposal terms remain human-reviewed.
