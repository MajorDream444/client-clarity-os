# Generic Client Blueprint Intake

Use this only when the client does not fit the Creator-Healer, Shakti System, or Media Engine archetypes.

## Opening

This intake captures the facts needed to diagnose the business, clarify the offer, identify the fastest path to revenue, and define the first useful build.

## Origin

- What is the story behind the business or project?
- Why does this work matter now?
- What has already been tried?

## Work / Authority

- What do you do, deliver, or solve?
- What proof or credibility already exists?
- What makes your approach meaningfully different?

## People / ICP

- Who is the best-fit customer?
- What problem do they already know they have?
- What makes them ready to buy?

## Offer / Revenue

- What has already made money?
- What is the strongest offer right now?
- What would make the offer easier to understand, trust, and buy?

## Resistance / Boundaries

- What should the business avoid building, selling, or promising?
- Where is the current process too manual, unclear, or fragile?

## Expression / Delivery

- What is the best first client-facing door?
- What assets, pages, forms, automations, or follow-up systems are needed?

## Vision

- What is the bigger operating system vision?
- What belongs later?

## Reality Check

- What assets, proof, audience, pricing, and operations already exist?
- What is missing before the next stage can work?

## Final Reflection

What is the simplest first build that would create clarity, revenue movement, and operational leverage?
