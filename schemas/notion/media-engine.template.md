# Media Engine Intake

Use this for Shaun-style clients: media, PR, cultural authority, client access, service packaging, verticals, recurring revenue, campaign revenue, white-label engines, fundability, and acquisition potential.

## Opening

This intake is built to turn founder access, media proof, relationships, campaign history, and cultural authority into a repeatable revenue engine.

Answer with the real business facts. The strongest blueprint will come from what has already worked.

## Origin

- What is the founder story behind the media engine?
- What cultural, industry, or relationship advantage does the company already have?
- What moments prove the market has trusted the brand?

## Work / Authority

- What services, campaigns, PR outcomes, media placements, or creative work have already been delivered?
- Which relationships or verticals create unfair access?
- What proof would matter to a buyer, partner, or retainer client?

## People / ICP

- Who is the highest-value buyer right now?
- Which verticals are most natural: artists, athletes, brands, venues, labels, agencies, founders, or investors?
- Who buys once, who buys repeatedly, and who could white-label the engine?

## Offer / Revenue

- What has already made money?
- Which offers can become retainers, campaign packages, or recurring infrastructure?
- Where is revenue leaking because the offer is unclear, too custom, or not packaged?

## Resistance / Boundaries

- What should the company stop selling or stop doing manually?
- What deals look exciting but create operational drag?
- What must stay premium, relationship-led, or founder-approved?

## Expression / Delivery

- What should be packaged as the front door?
- What should become the back-end engine?
- What needs a deck, case study, intake, client portal, or campaign tracker?

## Vision

- What would make this fundable, acquirable, or partner-ready?
- What would a real media operating system include over time?
- Which pieces should not be built in the first 30 days?

## Reality Check

- What assets exist: deck, testimonials, campaign data, media lists, case studies, pricing, CRM, content, or partner proof?
- What is missing before recurring revenue can be sold cleanly?

## Final Reflection

What is the simplest first build that turns proof, access, and relationships into a repeatable media revenue engine?
