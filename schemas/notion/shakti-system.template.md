# Shakti System Intake

Use this for Sheetal-style clients: Shakti Tantra, somatics, neuroscience, diaspora positioning, women's sovereignty, circles, retreats, sanctuary, and temple pathway.

## Opening

This intake is designed to hold the doctrine, lineage, embodied practice, and practical business reality of the Shakti System without flattening it into generic wellness copy.

Answer with the language you would trust if it were reflected back to the right women.

## Origin

- What lineage, lived experience, study, or initiation shaped this work?
- What does Shakti mean inside this system, in your own language?
- What pain, disconnection, or fragmentation is this work responding to?

## Work / Authority

- What do you actually guide women through?
- How do Tantra, somatics, neuroscience, and diaspora identity meet inside the work?
- What proof, client stories, circles, or retreats already show the work is alive?

## People / ICP

- Which women are ready for this now?
- What are they carrying in the body, nervous system, relationships, work, or identity?
- What does sovereignty mean for them in lived terms?
- Who is not ready or not aligned for this container?

## Offer / Revenue

- What offers, circles, sessions, retreats, or programs have already made money?
- Which pathway feels most natural to sell first?
- What wants to become a foundation, front door, retreat, or sanctuary path?

## Resistance / Boundaries

- What must be protected so the work does not become extractive, rushed, or diluted?
- Where does visibility, sales, or scale create resistance?
- What boundaries should the system enforce from the beginning?

## Expression / Delivery

- Where does your transmission feel strongest: writing, voice, teaching, ceremony, retreat, one-to-one, circle, or curriculum?
- What should the first client-facing door feel like?
- What language should be avoided?

## Vision

- What is the long-term temple, sanctuary, retreat, or community vision?
- Which pieces belong later, after the foundation is clear?

## Reality Check

- What audience, assets, testimonials, recordings, curriculum, pricing, or partner pathways already exist?
- What is missing before the system can carry demand?
- What should not be built yet?

## Final Reflection

What is the simplest structure that would let the medicine reach the right women without you carrying everything manually?
